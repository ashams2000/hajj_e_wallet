ABOUT US

Mohammed Ali Al-Mutlaq Sons Co. for Trading & Cont. (M. A. Al-Mutlaq Sons Co.) [Formerly Al-Mutlaq Est. for Trading & Cont.] is a 100% Saudi owned Company and is among one of high middle size business companies in Saudi Arabia.

Mohammed Ali Al-Mutlaq Sons Co. for Trading & Cont. (M. A. Al-Mutlaq Sons Co.) [Formerly Al-Mutlaq Est. for Trading & Cont.] is a 100% Saudi owned Company and is among one of high middle size business companies in Saudi Arabia. The Group is established by (Late) General Mohammed Ali Al-Mutlaq, former Governor General for Military Affairs of Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia and has been in business for over 50 years. The company is managed by Mr. Monther Al-Mutlaq in the capacity of Managing Director. Mr. Monther Al-Mutlaq is an young, energetic technocrat with a Bachelor Degree in Architectural Engineering.

The Group has several companies / divisions, that are actively involved in various business fields, such as: Motorcycles Division (Harley-Davidson of Saudi Arabia : Distributor for Harley-Davidson Motor Company – Harley-Davidson), Petroleum & Industrial Services Division (NDT Inspection Services) Industrial Material & Services Division, Real Estate Division, Agriculture Division, Solid Surface Division, Chemical Division, Investment Division etc.

Al-Mutlaq United Co. are part of M. A. Al-Mutlaq Sons Company. M. A. Al-Mutlaq Sons Co. has branch offices and showrooms in G.C.C. Countries: Bahrain, Qatar and major cities of Saudi Arabia – Dammam, Jeddah, Riyadh and Qassim. The Company is equipped with all requisite infrastructure facilities such as “State-of-the-Art” Showrooms, Well Maintained Fleet of Vehicles, Modern Up-to-date Tools & Instruments, Modern Office Facilities, Professional & Experienced Management, Strong & Skilled Workforce etc. for the successful operation of the various divisions and branches.

M. A. Al-Mutlaq Sons Co. supply products and services of various European and American Companies throughout Saudi Arabia and other G.C.C. Countries through its various divisions and affiliate companies. M. A. Al-Mutlaq Sons Co. enjoys the trust and support of a large customer base both in the Public and Private Sector in these countries.
